Is a McMansion good for a McLoan ?¶

by Daniel Cornea¶

Investigation Overview¶

The overall goal of this presentation is to look at the interdepencies of homeownership and borrowing money on the Prosper.com platform

Dataset Overview¶

The dataset that we look at is the Prosper.com operated by the Prosper Marketplace, Inc a peer to peer lending company

As you can see from the slide the distribution is about equal for people who own a home and for those who do not

Interaction between Debt to Income Ratio, Loan Amount and homeownership¶

The homeowners seem to be clustered in the lower right of the graph, meaning that they lower debt to income ratio and therefore might afford bigger loan amounts

To answer the question in the title it seems that owning a McMansion tends to land you a bigger McLoan hence more McNuggets....¶

This is only a visual of the data, in order to make our conlucison more reliable additional more rigourous statistical testing is needed.
